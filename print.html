Print Project

I enjoyed creating the additional art for the postcard print project. I included photos of my acrylic paintings in the project text descriptions and the cover postcard. I also enjoyed designing the cover postcard because I could incorporate my love of geometric shapes and composition that I use in my live work with the digital. Moreover, I used one of my favorite color palettes - purple, pink and deep blue - to unify the various projects. Creating harmony and order were paramount to my design in bringing together separate projects in a single, readable layout.
